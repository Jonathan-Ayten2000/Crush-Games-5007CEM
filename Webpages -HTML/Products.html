S.T.A.L.K.E.R

The S.T.A.L.K.E.R series of games is our take on what would happen if the Chernobyl accident, was something more, something much more horrifying but intreging. It is a FPS, Survival RPG where you'll will be dropped into "The Zone", the Chernonbyl exclusion zone around the the Chernoyl power plant and surronding area. Here you will find all matter of treasure and creature, to find and conquer. But beware, something much darker is at work.


Battlefield

The Battleifeld Series is a collection of FPS shooters where you play as oppsoing forces attempting to conquer regions of the globe. We have 3 games in this series: Battlefield 1, set in the First World War. Battlefield 2, were Russia, USA and other countries are in a global war against each other. Battlefield 3, our sequel to Battlefield 2 where China has entered the War and you play as an american soldier with infomration about a global terrorist plot that could send the planet into even more crisis. All games in our Battlefield series have a singleplayer campaign, co-op campaign and our highly popular Multiplayer mode. Where you and 63 others can play on large scale maps, batteling each other for Victory.
